Help page of the SMP Economy bot in Secret Family.
The username obscurus2808 and "Revanant Kingz" will be used to demonstrate both one word and multiple word usernames when calling for differnet commands.

ECONOMY

Simple commands used to add, look, and create an account.

code definition example
.new Creates a new account. It will prompt you for your Minecraft (XBox-Live Account Username) for our database.

KEEP IN MIND: The username will always be case-sensitive (capital letters matters) so be warned! 		Link to image if what it will look like.
.bal <player> Checks the <players> balance. Pretty self-explanitory. .bal obscurus2808,
.bal "Revanant Kingz"
The one with more than two words must have double-qoutes.
.give <player1> <player2> Where <player1> (you) will give to <player2> an amount of money to given. If <player1> doesn't have the money needed to give, it will automatically cancel the transaction. .give obscurus2808 "Revanant Kingz" 40

FOR ECONOMISTS

These are special commands for people who have the "Economist" role on the server. Reason for it to not make the prices inflated and making the prices super expensive for no reason becuase of someone having overpowered commands.

code definition example
.add <player> <amount> Will instantly add whatever <amount> the <player> will have. .add "Revanant Kingz" 400
.remove <player> <amount> Will instantly remove whatever <amount> the <player> will have. .remove "Revanant Kingz" 1000
.set <player> <default amount> Will set the <player> to it's default amount to whatever it has. This will delete the players original balance, to the amount you have set. .set obscurus2808 400
.del <player> Will instantly delete the <player> from our database. There will be a small warning and will not be reverted. .del obscurus2808
